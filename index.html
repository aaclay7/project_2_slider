• The Tesla Model S is the start to a revolution within the electric car industry.

  • This ground-breaking car is one of the most efficient and stylish cars on the road.

  • Unlike the hybrids, such as the Toyota Prius, the Model S is solely electric and does not require gasoline.

  • As of December 2014, Tesla strategically placed 119 Supercharge stations nationwide.

  • Two batteries can be installed to minimize charging time. This innovative vehicle can be ordered with three different types of batteries, which increase the horsepower and miles driven on one charge.

  • The engineers of the Model S went to extreme lengths to ensure that the car is aerodynamic in all aspects.

  • There are several innovative features on the Model S that provide a luxorious and comfortable experience.

  • From the 17-inch display to the auto-piolot feature, the Model S has become the Most Loved Car of 2014.

Tesla's advanced electric powertrain delivers exhilarating performance. Unlike a gasoline internal combustion engine with hundreds of moving parts, Tesla electric motors have only one moving piece: the rotor. As a result, Model S acceleration is instantaneous, silent and smooth. Step on the accelerator and in as little as 3.2 seconds Model S is travelling 60 miles per hour, without hesitation, and without a drop of gasoline. Model S is an evolution in automobile engineering.
